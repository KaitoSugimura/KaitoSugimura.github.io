About Me

Above all, video games are meant to just be one thing: Fun for everyone. - Satoru Iwata

Who am I?

On paper, I am a Software Engineer. But if you get to know me, you'll know I am just a hobbyist who enjoys programming and creating new things.

Growing up, I've always been into video games, especially ones created by the game company Nintendo. I remember the joy and fun I had playing games such as: Pokémon, Mario, Kirby, and many other famous titles. It was what let me dream of many different fantasies and solve various types of puzzles. I still remember to this day, the feeling of waiting till Christmas or my Birthday for my parents to buy me the newest game. It was truly what brought delight and happiness into my world.

That is why my dream is to become an engineer/developer that would do the same and bring a smile to another person's face. To be bluntly honest, until recently, I never really fully understood what it meant to be a software engineer. In fact I initially chose this degree out of spite thinking it was just computer related. But right now, I am glad to be where I am and I can confidently say, this is where I belong. Coding can be challenging or even frustrating at times, but the satisfaction of clicking compile and having no errors can never be beaten.

My definition of Engineer

A Scientist is someone who wishes to learn more about the world. To seek the truth by creating theories and testing hypotheses.
An Engineer is someone who utilizes that knowledge and discovery. To materialize science into something that can be used by the common good.

My goal as a Software Engineer is to help create a Game/Program/Product that would give off the same joy and happiness I once had as a child playing video games. Something that is fun for Everyone.
